ATLAUNCHER

Download, install, and set up ATLauncher (https://atlauncher.com/downloads).

Download either the CurseForge or the MultiMC version of the modpack, since ATLauncher supports both. Do not extract it.

Open ATLauncher, go to "Instances" on the right, and click "Import" in the top left corner.

Click "Browse", select the modpack .zip you downloaded, and import it.

Go back to ATLauncher, click "Settings" on the newly imported modpack, go to "Java/Minecraft", and ensure sufficient maximum RAM (6000-8000).
